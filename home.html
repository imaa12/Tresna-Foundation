The problem of hunger and malnutrition is a problem faced by many countries around the world. On the other hand, many items are wasted and not utilized every day, including in Bali. So far, food waste or food waste, especially in Bali, has occurred because many restaurants, hotels and supermarkets produce large amounts of food waste every day. In addition, many tourists who come to Bali also leave behind unfinished food, which is a significant source of food waste on the island.





FAQ


Questions that may be asked.

How do we donate?
After you log in to the account you created on this website, you can click the "Donate Now" button to take you to the donation Google Form.

If you still don't understand or have something to ask further, you can contact the contact listed on this website.

What can be donated?
You can donate food that is still suitable for consumption.

How can I become a volunteer?
After you have successfully log in to the account you have created on this web, you can click the "Be Volunteer" button to take you to the Google Form that must be filled in.
